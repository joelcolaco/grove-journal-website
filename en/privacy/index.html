Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 19, 2026

This draft summarizes how Grove Journal is expected to handle data. It should be reviewed before public launch.

Journal entries

Grove Journal stores journal entries on your device. Entries may include text, dates, moods, and timestamps. Grove Journal does not run its own cloud service for storing your journal entries.

Optional backup

If you choose to use backup features, backup files are stored in cloud accounts that you control, such as iCloud or Google Drive. Backup files may contain readable copies of your journal entries and related metadata.

Google Drive

When you connect Google Drive, Grove Journal requests access to files it creates or opens for the app. The app may store limited account display information on your device, such as your Google account email, name, and profile photo, so it can show which account is connected.

iCloud

When you use iCloud backup, backup files are stored in your own iCloud account and are subject to Apple's iCloud settings and terms.

Notifications

Grove Journal may ask for permission to send journal and backup reminders. Reminder settings are stored on your device.

Diagnostics

Grove Journal plans to use Sentry for crash and error diagnostics. Diagnostic reports may include app version, device information, operating system details, error messages, stack traces, and related context. Grove Journal should be configured to avoid sending journal entry content to diagnostic tools.

Subscriptions

Grove Journal plans to use RevenueCat to manage Premium subscriptions. Subscription processing may involve Apple, Google, and RevenueCat according to their terms and privacy practices.

Daily quotes

Grove Journal may request a daily quote from ZenQuotes. Journal entries are not sent to ZenQuotes.

Data deletion

You can delete local data by removing the app from your device, subject to your device platform's behavior. You can also delete backup files from your own iCloud or Google Drive account. Google Drive access can be revoked from your Google Account permissions page.

Contact

A custom-domain support and privacy email address will be added before public launch.
