Privacy

Your journal starts on your device.

Grove Journal does not run its own cloud service for storing journal entries. Optional backups are saved to cloud storage accounts that you control, such as iCloud or Google Drive.

Plain-language summary: no Grove Journal-hosted journal database, optional user-owned cloud backup, and diagnostic reporting only when configured for app reliability.

Premium

Automatic backup, when you want extra peace of mind.

Premium unlocks automatic backup to iCloud and Google Drive. Manual journaling, mood tracking, history, search, reminders, export/import, and manual backup/restore remain available unless product direction changes.
